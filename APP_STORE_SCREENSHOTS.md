- Real profit after entry fees, transport & all expenses
- Monthly profit charts and inventory value at a glance
- Works 100% offline — no account or signup needed
- Optional Google Sheets sync for spreadsheet lovers
- Multi-currency (USD, EUR, GBP)
- Backup & restore — never lose your data
